# WasteWarrior: A Food Waste Management Web App

WasteWarrior is an innovative web application designed to empower users to manage and minimize food waste. Utilizing Kotlin and JavaScript, it offers features to track food inventory, report waste, access educational resources, and engage in a community forum.

![WasteWarrior App Screenshot](./images/wastewarrior.png) _Screenshot of the WasteWarrior application_

## Features

### Food Inventory Management
- Track your food stocks and expiration dates.
- Receive alerts for food nearing its expiration date.

### Waste Tracking/Reporting
- Log and track food waste over time.
- Visualize waste statistics with user-friendly charts.

### Educational Resources
- Access tips and advice on reducing food waste.
- Explore articles, infographics, and videos.

### Community Forum
- Share tips, tricks, and success stories.
- Engage with a community of waste-conscious individuals.

## Technologies Used
- **Backend**: Kotlin
- **Frontend**: JavaScript
